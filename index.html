BetterBite: AI-Powered Grocery Nutrition Scanner

BetterBite is a professional AI nutrition assistant that analyzes grocery products in real-time. By scanning nutrition labels and ingredient lists, our advanced AI provides a Health Score from 1 to 10, helping users identify healthy grocery choices, avoid ultra-processed foods, and find better alternatives.

Key Features of BetterBite

  • Instant Nutrition Label Scanner: Decode complex food labels with a single photo.
  • AI Health Scoring: Scientific 1-10 ratings based on nutritional density and processing.
  • Ingredient Analysis: Identify hidden additives, sugars, and artificial sweeteners like Sorbitol.
  • Product Comparison: Compare multiple grocery items side-by-side to find the healthiest option.
  • Personalized History: Track your grocery scans and monitor your health journey over time.

Using Google Gemini Vision, BetterBite identifies ingredients and nutrition facts to calculate a precise health score based on food science benchmarks.
